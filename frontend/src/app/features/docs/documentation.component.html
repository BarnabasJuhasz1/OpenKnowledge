Using OpenKnowledge

OpenKnowledge is a free, open-source platform for literature reviews — discovering papers across major academic databases, scoring their relevance, and mapping how the research connects.

Getting started

  1. Open My Projects from the Dashboard and create a project.
  2. Inside your project, pick a feature from the left panel and describe what you are looking for in the search bar.
  3. Refine results with the OK-score weights, then save what matters to your Library.

Contribution Archetypes

OpenKnowledge automatically classifies academic research papers into 8 universal contribution archetypes based on their primary intellectual value. This classification is visible directly on paper cards, in side details panels, and can be used for advanced filtering of search results.
